Who We Are

ArmorSec Cyber Defense is founded by a U.S. military veteran with a passion for cybersecurity, firearms, and protecting the freedoms we value.

With a degree in cybersecurity and hands-on experience, our mission is to help small businesses — especially gun shops, ranges, and 2A-supporting organizations — stay secure in an increasingly hostile digital world.

We combine military-grade discipline with cutting-edge tech to defend your business and your customers.
